# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## 1. Objective

Launch **SamvidAI** as a credible, enterprise-ready AI platform for intelligent contract analysis, rapidly validating product–market fit (PMF) while building trust with legal and compliance stakeholders.

---

## 2. Target Customers

### Primary

* **Mid-to-large law firms** (corporate, M&A, litigation)
* **In-house legal teams** (tech, fintech, manufacturing)
* **Compliance & risk teams**

### Secondary

* Legal tech consultants
* Contract-heavy SMBs
* Legal BPO/KPO firms

---

## 3. Problem Statement

Legal teams face:

* Time-consuming contract review
* High error risk in clause identification
* Poor visibility across large contract repositories
* Limited AI solutions tailored to jurisdiction-specific legal language

---

## 4. Value Proposition

**SamvidAI** delivers:

* AI-powered clause extraction & risk detection
* OpticalRAG for scanned & legacy documents
* Explainable outputs aligned with legal reasoning
* Secure, privacy-first deployment options

**Outcome:** Faster reviews, lower risk, and scalable legal intelligence.

---

## 5. Product Positioning

> *“An intelligent contract analysis engine built for real legal workflows, not generic document AI.”*

Differentiators:

* OpticalRAG (text + scanned docs)
* Jurisdiction-aware analysis (India-first)
* Research-grade documentation (HLD/LLD/Data Reports)

---

## 6. MVP Scope (Launch v1)

* PDF/DOCX upload
* Clause detection (termination, indemnity, liability, governing law)
* Risk flags with explanations
* Exportable summaries
* Web UI (internal / pilot users)

---

## 7. Pricing & Packaging (Initial)

### Pilot Phase

* Free / invite-only
* NDA-based access

### Post-PMF

* **Starter**: Per-user/month
* **Team**: Per-seat + document volume
* **Enterprise**: Custom pricing, on-prem / private cloud

---

## 8. Distribution Channels

### Direct

* Founder-led outreach (LinkedIn, email)
* Legal tech communities
* Cold demos to law firms & startups

### Indirect (Later)

* Legal consultants
* System integrators
* Partnerships with compliance platforms

---

## 9. Marketing Strategy

### Phase 1 – Credibility

* Open GitHub repo with strong documentation
* Technical blogs (OpticalRAG, legal AI pitfalls)
* Demo videos

### Phase 2 – Demand

* Case studies from pilot users
* Webinars with legal professionals
* SEO: “AI contract review India”, “Legal AI Optical OCR”

---

## 10. Sales Motion

* High-touch demos
* Proof-of-value (POV) projects (2–4 weeks)
* Annual contracts post-pilot

---

## 11. Metrics & KPIs

* Pilot-to-paid conversion
* Avg. review time reduction
* Clause detection accuracy
* Active weekly users
* Retention (30/90 days)

---

## 12. Risks & Mitigation

| Risk                | Mitigation                |
| ------------------- | ------------------------- |
| Trust in AI outputs | Explainability, citations |
| Data privacy        | On-prem / private cloud   |
| Slow sales cycles   | Pilot-led adoption        |

---

## 13. Roadmap Alignment

* **Q1:** MVP + pilots
* **Q2:** Paid teams + analytics
* **Q3:** Enterprise features, integrations
* **Q4:** Expansion beyond contracts

---

## 14. Exit Criteria for PMF

* 5–10 paying teams
* Repeat usage without hand-holding
* Clear ROI acknowledged by users

---

*This GTM strategy is designed to evolve with validated learnings from pilots and early adopters.*
